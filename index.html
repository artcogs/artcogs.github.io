Welcome to the Artificial Cognitive Systems Group (ArtCogS)!

At ArtCogS, we explore the frontier where intelligent agents, multi-agent systems, and artificial cognition meet. Our research focuses on understanding and engineering cognitive behaviours in artificial systems through advanced AI methods, with a strong emphasis on Reinforcement Learning, simulation, and agent-based modelling.

As part of the Athena Research Center, our group collaborates across disciplines to design systems that can perceive, learn, reason, and act autonomously in complex environments. We conduct applied and theoretical research in areas such as autonomous robotics, adaptive decision-making, digital twins, cognitive architectures, and multi-agent cooperation.

Our mission is to advance the state of the art in artificial cognition and to develop intelligent systems that contribute to science, industry, and society.
