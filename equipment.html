Equipment

Infrastructure and Tools Supporting Our Research Activities

4×4 Autonomous Ground Vehicle with Robotic Arm

A rugged all-terrain robotic platform equipped with RGB/depth cameras, LiDAR, GPS, IMU, and a 6-DOF manipulator. It supports autonomous navigation, SLAM, sample collection, and field robotics research.

DJI AGRAS T30 – Agricultural UAV

A heavy-duty agricultural drone supporting automated spraying, fertiliser distribution, and large-scale field management with RTK precision and a 30L payload.

DJI Phantom 4 Multispectral

A drone equipped with RGB and multispectral cameras for crop analytics, vegetation indexing, and environmental monitoring with centimeter-level accuracy.

DJI Mavic 3T – Thermal UAV

A compact UAV with a high-resolution thermal camera, ideal for emergency response, fire detection, infrastructure inspection and night-time operations.

Drone Swarm – DJI Mini 3 Pro

A versatile drone swarm used for multi-agent experiments, aerial mapping, archaeology, climate research, and swarm intelligence algorithms.

Small-Scale Ground Robot Swarm

A fleet of small autonomous ground vehicles for reinforcement learning, multi-agent coordination, sensor fusion, navigation experiments, and AI-driven mobility research.

Edge AI Computing Units

High-performance edge devices supporting computer vision, deep learning inference, IoT integration, and robotics automation in real-world deployments.

High-Performance Computing Server

A multi-GPU server featuring 10× NVIDIA RTX A6000 GPUs and 1 TB RAM— supporting large-scale deep reinforcement learning, digital twins, image processing, and scientific simulations.

Specialised Cameras

Our lab includes depth, thermal, multispectral, and LiDAR-enabled cameras used for SLAM, 3D reconstruction, thermal inspection, crop diagnostics, XR interaction, and robotics navigation.

Extended Reality (XR) Headsets

Our XR suite includes HoloLens 2, Magic Leap One, and Oculus Quest 2— supporting immersive training, museum experiences, digital twins, teleoperation, and collaborative mixed-reality design.

Motion Tracking Sensors

Including Leap Motion controllers for hand tracking and interaction, used in XR, robotics teleoperation, and gesture-based interfaces.
